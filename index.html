About Me

Hi, I'm Ashwani

After decades of deadlines and meetings, I finally traded the office for the open road. This site is my little corner of the internet to share what I'm seeing, doing, and learning in this next chapter.

I'm passionate about photography, getting lost in a good book, fly fishing, and planning the next adventure. If it involves wide-open spaces and no cell service, I'm probably already on my way there.
