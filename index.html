About Me

Hi, I'm Ashwani

After years of full schedules and crowded inboxes, I'm finally making space for the things that have always been waiting. This site is my little corner of the internet — somewhere to write, reflect, and stay connected with the people who matter most.

I'm drawn to wide-open spaces, cold mountain streams, long drives with no destination, and the kind of books that make you put them down just to think. If any of that sounds familiar, you're in the right place.
